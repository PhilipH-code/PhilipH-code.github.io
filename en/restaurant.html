The house

Restaurant by the English Garden

450 seats, three terraces and a kitchen that cooks all day.

Café Reitschule is a restaurant, café and bar in the historic university riding school at Königinstraße 34 in Munich-Schwabing. Warm kitchen all day, 450 seats indoors and on three terraces, right by the English Garden.

Inside & outside

Salon, garden,
terrace

Inside, warm colours and plenty of green welcome you, with brass under tall windows. From the dining room you look into the old riding hall. 180 seats spread across restaurant, orangery and lounge.

Outside you sit under old trees, a stone’s throw from the English Garden. The three terraces are among the finest spots in Schwabing. At lunchtime and in the evening.

180 seats inside 270 on the terraces Warm kitchen all day

Kitchen

Classics,
done well

Executive chef Markus Kainz cooks what Munich loves to eat: Wiener Schnitzel, boeuf tartare, club sandwich. With wines and champagne from a well-kept list.

Wiener Schnitzel

The classic of the house, award-winning and never off the menu. Veal, clarified butter, nothing else.

Boeuf Tartare

Best dressed the classic way, with toasted bread. A dish for long evenings at the bar.

Club Sandwich

Proof that a sandwich in Schwabing can have real stature. Ordered at noon and late at night alike.

Cakes & Pastries

From our own pâtissier, in the vitrine every afternoon. For your coffee, or as the reason for one.

A day in the house

From the first coffee
to the blue hour

Morning

On weekends and holidays the house opens at 9 with breakfast, à la carte until 1 pm. On Sundays the grand brunch at Moro Mou joins in, booked via OpenTable.

Noon

On weekdays the kitchen opens at 11:30 for lunch. Coming from the English Garden, you are under the parasols of the Königinterrasse within two minutes in summer.

Afternoon

The hour of the vitrine: cakes and pastries from our own pâtissier, coffee and a seat in the orangery. The calmest time of the house.

Evening

From 5 pm the bar pours for the champagne happy hour; the kitchen serves warm food until 9:30 pm, Sundays until 6 pm. After that the evening belongs to drinks in the lounge.

Good to know

Questions for
the restaurant

Are dogs welcome in the restaurant?

Yes. Your dog is welcome, indoors and on the terraces.

Can I get a table without a reservation?

Often, yes; the house seats 450. In the evening and on weekends we recommend reserving; for the same day it is best to call: +49 89 38 88 76 0.

Can I sit outside in cooler weather?

Yes. The Panoramaterrasse is covered and heated, reliable from spring into autumn.

When is breakfast served?

Saturdays, Sundays and holidays from 9 am to 1 pm, à la carte on the ground floor. On Sundays there is also the brunch at Moro Mou.

How late does the kitchen cook?

Warm food all day: Monday to Saturday until 9:30 pm, Sundays and holidays until 6 pm.

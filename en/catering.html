Good to know

Questions about
catering

Where do you deliver?

To Munich and the surrounding area. For larger events we travel further afield. Just ask us for your location.

From how many guests does catering make sense?

From dinner at home to a reception for several hundred guests. Tell us about your occasion and we’ll find the right format.

How much notice do you need?

The earlier the better, especially in high season. We still try to make short-notice requests work.

Do you bring staff and equipment?

Yes. Trained staff, experienced decorators and the right equipment are part of it. On request we also plan the supporting programme.

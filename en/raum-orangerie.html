Good to know

Questions about
Orangerie

How many guests fit in?

Orangerie: up to 70 guests seated, up to 90 for a reception. The floor area is 76 m².

What occasions suit this room?

Especially Weddings, Christenings & family celebrations, Receptions, Brunch parties. We arrange the room to suit your occasion.

How do I enquire without obligation?

With an email to contact@cafe-reitschule.de or by phone at +49 89 38 88 76 0. We reply quickly and are glad to advise you personally.

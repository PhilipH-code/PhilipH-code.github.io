Questions about
Lounge

How many guests fit in?

Lounge: up to 72 guests seated, up to 100 for a reception. The floor area is 63 m².

What occasions suit this room?

Especially Birthdays, Aperitif receptions, Afterwork, Private dinner. We arrange the room to suit your occasion.

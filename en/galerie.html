The house in pictures

The Gallery

Summer light on the terrace, the salon in the evening. And plates, just before they go to the table.

What you are seeing

One house,
three faces

Inside

Warm colours, brass and plenty of green under tall windows: the grand dining room, the orangery with palms and glass, the lounge with Chesterfield sofas. 180 seats that look more like a living room than a restaurant.

Outside

Three terraces with 270 seats: the Königinterrasse under old trees by the street, the covered Panoramaterrasse looking into the green, the Sonnenterrasse above the rooftops with evening sun.

From the kitchen

The award-winning Wiener Schnitzel, classics from the bar and cakes from our own pâtissier. Photographed the way it is served, no retouching.

Good to know

Questions about
the pictures

Can I view the house before a celebration?

Gladly. The event team will arrange an appointment and show you the rooms in person: contact@cafe-reitschule.de.

Where do I find pictures of the individual rooms?

Every room has its own page with a gallery and seating plans. The interactive floor plan on Rooms & Celebrations gives you the overview.

Are the photos up to date?

Yes, all pictures are from the house and are added to regularly. What you see here is what you see on site.

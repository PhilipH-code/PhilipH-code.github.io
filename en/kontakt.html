Good to know

Questions before
your visit

How do I get to Café Reitschule?

Take the U-Bahn to Giselastraße (U3/U6); from there it’s a few minutes on foot. The address is Königinstraße 34, 80802 Munich. By bike, park it right in front of the house.

Is there parking nearby?

There is street parking around Königinstraße, but it is limited. The U-Bahn or a bike is often the easier way in.

Are dogs welcome?

Yes. Your dog is welcome, indoors and on the terraces.

Are you open on holidays?

On Sundays and holidays we are here from 9 am to 7 pm. Breakfast is served until 1 pm.

Do I need a reservation?

Recommended, especially in the evening and at weekends. The easiest way is online via OpenTable. For the same day, a quick call is best: +49 89 38 88 76 0.

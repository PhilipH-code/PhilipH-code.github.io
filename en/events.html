The planning

What would you
care for?

For years we have hosted celebrations for Munich law firms, broadcasters and insurers, as well as weddings, christenings and milestone birthdays. The range runs from the dinner after your registry-office wedding to the themed party for a company anniversary.

What matters most is you. You should welcome your guests at ease and be a guest yourself. Our event team takes care of everything else.

Kitchen

Finger food, buffet or set menu, composed by our kitchen team.

Bar

Classic cocktails, fine wines and a well-rehearsed bar service.

Rooms

Individual room design with a wide choice of furniture.

Décor

Styling through our partner companies, for every occasion and theme.

Music

Experienced DJs or bands to match your evening.

Technology

Stage, projector and conference service, tailored to your needs.

Good to know

Questions about
the venue

How many guests can you host?

From 20 to 450 guests. Four rooms and three terraces can be used individually or in combination.

Can I hire the house exclusively?

Yes. You can have individual rooms or the whole house to yourself. Our event team finds the right layout for your occasion.

Which occasions suit the venue?

Weddings, birthdays, company parties, Christmas parties and conferences. In summer as in winter.

Is there equipment for talks and music?

Yes. Sound and presentation equipment as well as a DJ can be arranged through us. Tell us what your evening needs.

How do I enquire?

Write to contact@cafe-reitschule.de or call +49 89 38 88 76 0. We reply quickly and advise you personally.

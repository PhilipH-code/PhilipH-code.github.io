Good to know

The most common
questions

How do I reserve for an event?

Online via OpenTable, around the clock. For the same day it’s best to give us a quick call: +49 89 38 88 76 0. Every event on this page has its own reservation link.

Can I hire the Reitschule privately?

Yes. Four rooms and three terraces hold 20 to 450 guests. Our event team is happy to advise you at contact@cafe-reitschule.de.

Does the restaurant stay open during events?

Usually, yes. On some days the house closes early for private functions. You’ll find the current dates under What’s On.

Is there a dress code?

No. Most guests like to dress up anyway.

What happens when it rains?

Not much. Two of the three terraces are covered, and 180 seats wait inside.

Are children welcome?

Yes, very much so. There is a dedicated children’s menu, and the Sunday brunch has its own prices for young guests.

Breakfast in Schwabing

Mornings
by the garden

At weekends and on holidays we serve breakfast à la carte from 9 am to 1 pm. After that the kitchen takes over with the full menu.

On Sundays the brunch buffet at Moro Mou joins in. Those seats are booked exclusively via OpenTable.

To Sunday brunch

Lunch

Midday
under the trees

Monday to Friday from 11:30. A quick plate on your break or a long table in the shade of the chestnut trees, both work. The kitchen cooks right through, with no fixed meal times.

Classics like our award-winning Wiener Schnitzel, alongside dishes of the season. On weekends, midday follows straight on from breakfast.

In the evening

The evening
at dusk

As the light over the garden softens, the bar fills up. Warm kitchen until 21:30, cocktails and champagne into the late evening.

An aperitif before dinner or a long evening with friends: Monday to Saturday the Reitschule stays open until 23:00.

Gift Cards

Good hours make a fine gift. On good paper, not as a PDF.

The gift

A piece of the Reitschule,
to give away

Gift vouchers for everything this house does: breakfast with friends or dinner for two. Champagne without a special occasion counts too. What it becomes is up to the person you give it to.

The vouchers are also valid next door at Moro Mou, our Greek restaurant in the house.

It’s this simple

Two ways
to your voucher

01

In the house

During opening hours, right at the bar. The bar team issues your voucher on the spot, and you take it with you.

See opening hours
02

By post

We are happy to send the voucher to your home. An email or a quick call is all it takes.

Request a voucher

Good to know

  • Vouchers are available only in the house or by post, not online.
  • Cash redemption is not possible.
  • The voucher is not valid on New Year’s Eve.
  • On days with private functions the house may close early. You’ll find the dates under What’s On.

Good to know

Questions about
gift cards

Can I choose the amount freely?

Yes. These are value vouchers; you set the amount when you buy.

How do I get a voucher?

Straight from the bar during opening hours, issued on the spot. Or by post: an email to contact@cafe-reitschule.de or a quick call is all it takes.

How long is the voucher valid?

At least the statutory three years to the end of the year. And we are not petty about older vouchers, just ask.

Where can I redeem it?

Throughout the house and next door at Moro Mou, our Greek restaurant. It is only not valid on New Year’s Eve.

Can it be paid out in cash?

No, cash redemption is not possible. Any remaining balance naturally stays on the voucher.

Welcome

The right place,
at any hour

Between the English Garden and Leopoldstraße stands a house for nearly every occasion: weekend breakfast from 9 am, lunch from 11:30, cakes from our own pâtissier in the afternoon, cocktails at the bar in the evening. 180 seats inside, 270 outside on three terraces.

This is where Schwabing sits at one table: neighbours beside travellers, families beside walkers with and without a dog. The award-winning Wiener Schnitzel is always on the menu; on Sundays the brunch at Moro Mou joins in. Many come for an hour. Most stay longer.
